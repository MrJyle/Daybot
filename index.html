    Daybot Commands List:

    Welcome to the Daybot Command List. Here you will find all the commands that you can use with Daybot!


    School Commands

    - Day

    Lists current school Day

    Usage: | .day |

    - Schedule

    Shows Schedule based on Class role

    Usage: | .schedule |

    - Setday

    Sets current school day (requires Mod)

    Usage: | .setday {day} |


    Fun Commands

    - Rate

    Daybot rates you!

    Usage: | .rate {rater} |

    - PowerOfAOJ

    Usage: | .powerofaoj {message} |


    Action Commands

    - Hug

    Hug a member.

    Usage: | .hug {member} |

    - HoldHands

    Holdhands with a member.

    Usage: | .holdhands {member} |

    - Slap

    Slap a member.

    Usage: | .slap {member} |

    - Tickle

    Tickle a member.

    Usage: | .tickle {member} |

    - Pout

    Pout at a member.

    Usage: | .pout {member} |

    - Kiss

    Kiss a member.

    Usage: | .kiss {member} |

    - Shoot

    Shoot a member.

    Usage: | .shoot {member} |
